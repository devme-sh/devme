## Context

What forces are at play. The problem we're trying to solve, the constraints we're working under, the audience we care about. One to three paragraphs.

## Decision

What we decided to do, stated as an imperative. One paragraph; concrete; verb-led.

## Consequences

Positive and negative consequences of the decision. What this lets us do, what it costs us, what it locks us out of. Use sub-headers if helpful.

## Alternatives considered

The other plausible choices and why we rejected each. One short paragraph per alternative. This is the most important section for a future reader — it answers "why didn't they just do X?"
